  • Developing the right trader mindset
  • Position sizing and capital protection
  • Avoiding common mistakes

  • Proven setups used by professionals
  • Entry, Exit & Stop-loss techniques
  • Live market case studies
  • Tools & platforms used by active traders

✅ Module 6: Investing for Long-Term Wealth

  • Value investing vs growth investing
  • Building a long-term portfolio
  • Compounding strategies
  • SIPs, Mutual Funds & diversification

✅ Module 7: Futures, Options & Derivatives (Basics)

  • Understanding derivatives
  • When to use Options and Futures
  • Hedging vs Speculation
  • Simple option strategies
